中西合参 (East-West synthesis) is **not** stacking several divination systems to squeeze out one "more accurate" verdict. It puts the same relationship in front of several **different mirrors** and reads what each one reflects, where they echo one another, and where they talk past each other. The synthesis layer does **zero new calculation** — every system's chart is produced by its own casting script; my job is only to orchestrate and read across them.

**Who is present and who is absent (stated honestly)**:

- **natal (Western synastry) · degraded**: UNK's hour is unknown, so the script casts at 12:00. This means **UNK's houses, Ascendant, and UNK's Moon position are all unreliable**; therefore anything touching "house overlays" (宫位重叠) or "Ascendant-related" (上升相关) is **unavailable** and I will not cite it. I use only the **planet-to-planet aspects that do not depend on the birth hour** (Sun, Venus, Mars, and ABC's Moon — ABC's hour is known, so her Moon is reliable).
- **八字 (BaZi) · available**: fully in play. But because **UNK's hour is unknown, only three pillars (year/month/day) are cast — no hour pillar (时柱)**, so the comparison is anchored at the **day pillar (日柱)**.
- **紫微 (Zi Wei Dou Shu) · absent**: 紫微 has **no two-person mode**, so it is **skipped** and not included. Nowhere below will you find a 紫微 star or palace claim — that would be pure fabrication.
- **宿曜 (Xiu Yao / lunar-mansion astrology) · available**: fully in play (宿曜 needs only the birth date; an unknown hour degrades it **not at all**).

Of the four mirrors, **three are reflecting and one is absent** this time — and the three present ones differ in depth: natal is a **half-mirror, degraded**. This has to be on the table first; every conclusion below is read under this premise.

### Divergence (presented honestly): who carries the harder end of this relationship?

This is the one most worth laying open — the two mirrors give an **inconsistent** direction on "who has the harder time":

- **八字's direction**: UNK's day-stem is ABC's **七杀**, and on the stems "B controls A." By 八字, **the one being pressed, being commanded, is ABC** — UNK applies 七杀-style pressure onto ABC, and ABC is on the receiving end.
- **宿曜's direction**: **UNK-looking-at-ABC = 危** — by 宿曜, the one being activated, stirred, feeling the "taking-a-risk" sense is instead **UNK**; while **ABC-looking-at-UNK = 成**, ABC's side leaning toward harvesting results.

The two mirrors **do not agree**: 八字 says ABC bears more of the pressure, 宿曜 says UNK bears more of the risk-feeling. **I will not smooth this over, nor take a majority vote.** The more faithful reading is that each has caught a real layer: ABC genuinely feels UNK's "pushing, pressing" 七杀 pressure, and UNK, up close against ABC's people-reading, soft-outside-fierce-inside intensity, genuinely gets stirred and pushed to face reality. **Both kinds of strain exist at once** — different layers, different felt sense. This directional mismatch is exactly what the two should spell out to each other: **you think you're giving way; the other may feel he's taking a risk.**
