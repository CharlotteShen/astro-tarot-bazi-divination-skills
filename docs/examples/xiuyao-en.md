## ③ 三九相性 the three-nine compatibility (stated BOTH WAYS — the distinctive core of xiuyao)

The two are **危成组** (the Danger–Achievement group), at **中距离 (medium distance** — the
density of ordinary contact: neither glued together every hour nor so far that the tension is
diluted). The dynamic of 危成组 is: **heterogeneous mutual stimulation; the 成 (Achievement)
side reaps results, the 危 (Danger) side carries the risk — collaboration beats idle comfort.**
The crucial thing is that within one and the same relationship, **the two experience it in
opposite ways**:

**Direction rule (never reversed)**: 三九 relationships are directional. In "A looking at B ＝ X,"
X is where B falls on **A's** chart — i.e. **B's role relative to A** — and A's experience
follows from that.

- **甲方看乙方＝成 (ABC looking at XYZ ＝ 成 / Achievement)** — i.e. 心宿看虚宿＝成: XYZ (虚宿)
  lands on the **「成」position** of ABC's (心宿) chart, so **the results are reaped on ABC's
  side**. That is, **ABC grows and gains through the relationship with XYZ**: XYZ's sensitivity
  and aesthetic push ABC toward new vision, new work, new chances to get things done. In this
  bond ABC is the one "nourished into achievement" — the felt sense leans toward solid gain.

- **乙方看甲方＝危 (XYZ looking at ABC ＝ 危 / Danger)** — i.e. 虚宿看心宿＝危: ABC (心宿) lands
  on the **「危」position** of XYZ's (虚宿) chart, so **the risk and the being-stirred are carried
  on XYZ's side**. That is, **XYZ is activated — and also unsettled — by ABC**: ABC's ability to
  see through people, that warm-outside/intense-inside intensity, keeps pulling XYZ out of the
  spinning thought-loop and forcing them to face reality and own their choices. In this bond XYZ
  is the one "spurred, and also jolted" — the felt sense is tension and growth at once.

**The one line to remember for this pairing**: same relationship — **ABC feels they are
reaping results, XYZ feels they are taking a risk.** It is not right-vs-wrong; the two simply
feel the *same thing* differently by nature. A 危成 pair should not chase "peaceful stillness";
they do best doing something **goal-directed that requires coordination** — that is when the
two spark most; pure idleness is where this tension gets misread as friction.
