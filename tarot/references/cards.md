# The 78 cards — keyword reference (EN/中文)

Schema: `| # | Card | 中文名 | Suit | Element | Astro | Upright | Reversed |`. Keywords are hooks for
synthesis (see `reading-method.md`), not scripts to recite. Majors carry traditional Golden Dawn
astrological correspondences; minors carry their suit element (Wands=Fire 权杖, Cups=Water 圣杯,
Swords=Air 宝剑, Pentacles=Earth 星币) — the "Astro" bridge for minors is elemental only.
