# Spreads — when to use, what each position means

Choose the smallest spread that honors the question. All draws come from `scripts/draw.py` — never pick
cards by hand. (常用牌阵；所有抽牌必须用脚本。)

## single — Single Card (1)
Daily card, quick check-in, one focused question. **Guidance**: the energy to work with today/here.

## three — Three-Card (3)
The general-purpose spread. **Past**: what led here. **Present**: the heart of it now. **Future**: where
the current trajectory points (a trajectory, not a verdict).

## relationship — Relationship (5)
Any bond (romantic, friend, family, work). **You**: your stance/contribution. **The other**: theirs (read
with humility — it's their energy as it meets this bond, not their whole self). **The connection**: the
chemistry itself. **The challenge**: the friction to own. **Direction**: where it can grow.

## decision — Decision (5)
A fork in the road. **Heart of the matter**: what this choice is really about. **Path A** / **Path B**:
the energy of each option (map A/B explicitly with the querent before reading). **What you're not
seeing**: the blind spot. **Advice**: how to choose well — the reading never chooses for them.

## horseshoe — Horseshoe (7)
A situation unfolding over time. Distant past → Recent past → Present → Near future → External
influences → Advice → Likely outcome (again: trajectory, not fate).

## cycle — Monthly Cycle (4)
The month ahead as a lunar arc. **Seed (new moon)**: what to begin. **Action (first quarter)**: the
effort/friction. **Culmination (full moon)**: what comes to light. **Release (last quarter)**: what to
let go.

## celtic — Celtic Cross (10)
The deep dive for complex situations. Present · Challenge · Conscious goal · Foundation · Recent past ·
Near future · Self · Environment · Hopes and fears · Outcome. Read the axes together: 1–2 (the knot),
3–4 (conscious vs root), 7–8 (inside vs outside), 9–10 (the fear inside the ending).
