# Signs — modern-psychological keywords

Each sign: element + modality, core motivation, gift, shadow. Raw material for synthesis, not lines
to recite.

## Aries
- **element/modality:** Fire / Cardinal
- **motivation:** to initiate, assert selfhood, act on instinct.
- **gift:** courage, directness, fresh starts.
- **shadow:** impatience, combativeness, acting before thinking.

## Taurus
- **element/modality:** Earth / Fixed
- **motivation:** to secure stability, savor the physical world, build something lasting.
- **gift:** steadiness, sensory presence, patient persistence.
- **shadow:** stubbornness, resistance to change, attachment to comfort over growth.

## Gemini
- **element/modality:** Air / Mutable
- **motivation:** to gather and exchange information, connect ideas and people, stay curious.
- **gift:** wit, adaptability, fluent communication.
- **shadow:** scattered focus, restlessness, surface-level engagement that avoids depth.

## Cancer
- **element/modality:** Water / Cardinal
- **motivation:** to nurture, protect emotional bonds, create a felt sense of home.
- **gift:** empathy, intuitive care, loyalty.
- **shadow:** moodiness, defensiveness, clinging to the past or to people out of fear of loss.

## Leo
- **element/modality:** Fire / Fixed
- **motivation:** to express a vital, creative self and be seen authentically.
- **gift:** warmth, generosity, magnetic confidence.
- **shadow:** need for constant admiration, pride that resists feedback, dramatizing to be noticed.

## Virgo
- **element/modality:** Earth / Mutable
- **motivation:** to refine, be useful, bring order and competence to daily life.
- **gift:** discernment, diligence, practical problem-solving.
- **shadow:** perfectionism, self-criticism, anxious over-control of details.

## Libra
- **element/modality:** Air / Cardinal
- **motivation:** to harmonize, weigh perspectives, build fair and beautiful relationships.
- **gift:** diplomacy, aesthetic sense, collaborative fairness.
- **shadow:** conflict-avoidance, people-pleasing, indecision from over-weighing options.

## Scorpio
- **element/modality:** Water / Fixed
- **motivation:** to merge deeply, uncover hidden truth, transform through intensity.
- **gift:** psychological depth, loyalty, regenerative resilience.
- **shadow:** control through secrecy, jealousy, all-or-nothing power struggles.

## Sagittarius
- **element/modality:** Fire / Mutable
- **motivation:** to seek meaning, expand horizons, pursue truth through experience.
- **gift:** optimism, philosophical breadth, adventurous honesty.
- **shadow:** dogmatism, restlessness, overpromising or overlooking inconvenient details.

## Capricorn
- **element/modality:** Earth / Cardinal
- **motivation:** to achieve mastery, build durable structures, earn respect through responsibility.
- **gift:** discipline, strategic patience, integrity under pressure.
- **shadow:** rigidity, workaholism, equating self-worth with status or achievement.

## Aquarius
- **element/modality:** Air / Fixed
- **motivation:** to innovate, champion the collective, stand apart for a principle.
- **gift:** originality, egalitarian vision, intellectual independence.
- **shadow:** detachment, contrarianism, fixed ideology that resists personal feedback.

## Pisces
- **element/modality:** Water / Mutable
- **motivation:** to dissolve boundaries, empathize universally, transcend the literal.
- **gift:** compassion, imagination, spiritual and artistic sensitivity.
- **shadow:** escapism, blurred boundaries, self-sacrifice that slides into martyrdom.
