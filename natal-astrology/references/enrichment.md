# Enrichment layer — derived points (v1.1.3)

An OPTIONAL deeper layer. Surface on a drill-down or when a contact is notably tight — do NOT dump the
whole layer into every default reading. Never cite a point not in the chart's `enrichment` data.

## What each means
- **Sect** (day/night) — the chart's basic temperament; sets the lot formulas.
- **Part of Fortune** — where ease, flow, body/livelihood and a sense of flourishing land.
- **Part of Spirit** — where conscious will, vocation and intention flow.
- **Part of Eros** — where desire and passionate engagement land.
- **Sun/Moon midpoint** — the core "self" blend; planets on it colour the whole identity.
- **Occupied midpoints** — a third planet on a pair's midpoint expresses that pair's blend ("fated combination").
- **Declination parallels** — hidden, out-of-sign bonds: parallel ≈ conjunction, contra-parallel ≈ opposition.
- **Vertex** — "fated encounters" / turning-point sensitivity; reads like an extra angle (anti-vertex = opposite).

## Asteroids & Black Moon Lilith (v1.2.2)
Four asteroid archetypes + Black Moon Lilith, each with sign/degree/house and its tight (≤2°) contacts
to the natal planets/angles (`asteroid_contacts`). A supporting layer — surface on a drill-down or when a
contact is tight; don't dump all five into a default reading.
- **Ceres** — nurture and its cycles: how we mother and are mothered, food/body, loss and return.
- **Pallas** — pattern-wisdom: strategy, creative intelligence, seeing the design, justice.
- **Juno** — committed partnership: what we need in a mate, equality, the terms of "we".
- **Vesta** — devotion and focus: the tended flame, the sacred, sexuality as sublimation.
- **Black Moon Lilith** (mean apogee) — raw, rejected instinct: the taboo, the part that won't be tamed
  or compromised. Read most sharply by sign/house and by a tight contact to a personal point.

## Reading
Weave ≥2 factors, ~70% plain language. Treat lots/vertex as supporting points, not headlines, unless tight
or asked about. Two-sided, empower, no fatalism.

## Reliability (unknown birth time → noon)
- **Robust (usable):** planet declinations; planet–planet midpoints; parallels.
- **Flag (time-dependent):** the lots, the vertex, the house of any derived point (including asteroid/
  Lilith houses), any asteroid/Lilith contact to ASC or MC, and anything Moon-involving (Sun/Moon
  midpoint, Moon parallels). Never present noon as the real time.
