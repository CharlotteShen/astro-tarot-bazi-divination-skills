# Davison & Marks — relationship charts read like natals

These are extra outputs of the synastry mode, computed locally (no astro.com). Read each like a natal
chart, but interpret it as described. Inherits all synastry guardrails (no score, both people have
agency, hard contacts = growth not fate, never reference a factor not in the data).

## Descriptions (show the relevant one when the user picks)
- **Composite (standalone)** — "the relationship as its own *abstract* entity (midpoint of both charts'
  positions)." Its Sun/Moon/Ascendant + tightest aspects = the relationship's character. Houses are
  whole-sign; its Ascendant is time-dependent.
- **Davison** — "the relationship as a *lived* entity — a real moment in time and space." Has a real
  Ascendant/MC/houses. Read its Sun/Moon/Ascendant + tightest aspects as the relationship's core dynamic.
- **Marks · Natal × Composite** — "how *you* personally mesh with the relationship-as-an-entity."
- **Marks · Natal × Davison** *(default)* — "how you interact with the *lived* relationship — more
  grounded/event-like."
- **Marks · Canonical (Davison-of-Davison)** — "the relationship as felt *from your point of view*, as
  its own chart." Emphasize its **Sun** (your attitude / direction of growth in the relationship) and
  **Moon** (your emotional needs / where it soothes or strains you).

## Reading method
1. Confirm both charts + which output the user wants (composite / Davison / Marks). For Marks, confirm
   the mode (default Natal × Davison).
2. Read the chosen chart like a natal: lead with the Sun/Moon/Ascendant and the tightest aspects, weave
   ≥2 factors per claim, ~70% plain language.
3. For Marks comparison modes, synthesize the natal↔relationship inter-aspects + overlays (which area of
   the relationship the person most activates / is activated by).
4. Two-sided; both people have agency; no verdict, no score.

**Relationship type:** read every chart above through the chosen type (romantic / friendship / family /
work) per `relationship-frameworks.md` — e.g. romantic weights its Venus/Mars & 5th/7th themes; friendship
its Mercury/Jupiter & 3rd/11th; family its Moon/Saturn & 4th/10th; work its Saturn/Mercury/Mars & 6th/10th.

**Naming & perspective:** as in `synastry-method.md` — name both people (A/B) and stay third-person by
default (never mix "you"/"he/she"); switch to "you-perspective" only on request, after confirming whose chart is whose.

## Birth-time handling (reliability note)
If a birth time is unknown, the chart is computed with a **labeled local-noon (12:00) assumption**.
State this, then flag what's unreliable: **Ascendant, MC, houses, exact Moon degree, and anything
derived** (overlays; Davison/Marks angles & houses; composite ASC/houses). **Reliable:** most planet
positions and planet–planet aspects; the Moon's sign. Never present noon as the real birth time.
