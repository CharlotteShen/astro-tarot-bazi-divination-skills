# Aspects — angular relationships between bodies

Aspects show how two planetary functions interact. Tighter orb = louder. v1 uses the five major
aspects only.

## Major aspects
- **conjunction (0°):** fusion — the two functions blend and amplify; can't separate them.
- **sextile (60°):** ease/opportunity — cooperation that needs activation.
- **square (90°):** tension/friction — productive conflict that drives growth.
- **trine (120°):** flow/talent — natural ease, can be taken for granted.
- **opposition (180°):** polarity — pull between two needs; integration through awareness.

## Orb conventions (v1)
- Conjunction / opposition / square / trine: up to 8°.
- Sextile: up to 6°.
- Aspects to ASC/MC: up to 6°.
- Tighter orb = stronger; ≤3° is a "loud" signal for triage.

## Reading guidance
- A square is not "bad" nor a trine "good" — name the dynamic, not a verdict.
- Always read an aspect as two specific planets in specific signs/houses, never generically.
