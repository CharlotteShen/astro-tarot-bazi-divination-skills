# Houses — life areas

Each house: the life area it governs and the keyword themes a planet there activates. Whole-sign or
Placidus per the chart's `house_system`.

## House 1
- **area:** self, body, persona, how you start things and meet the world.
- **themes:** identity, appearance, initiative, first impressions.

## House 2
- **area:** resources, values, self-worth, money you earn.
- **themes:** material security, possessions, self-esteem, what you value.

## House 3
- **area:** mind, siblings, local environment, communication, short trips.
- **themes:** learning, speech, curiosity, everyday connections.

## House 4
- **area:** home, roots, family, the inner/private foundation.
- **themes:** belonging, ancestry, emotional security, domestic life.

## House 5
- **area:** creativity, romance, play, children, self-expression.
- **themes:** joy, performance, courtship, creative risk-taking.

## House 6
- **area:** work, health, daily routine, service, habits.
- **themes:** discipline, maintenance, wellbeing, practical competence.

## House 7
- **area:** partnership, committed relationships, the significant other.
- **themes:** mutuality, contracts, balance, the relational mirror.

## House 8
- **area:** intimacy, shared resources, transformation, depth, taboo.
- **themes:** merging, crisis and rebirth, vulnerability, hidden power.

## House 9
- **area:** meaning, travel, higher learning, belief, the big picture.
- **themes:** philosophy, exploration, faith, the search for truth.

## House 10
- **area:** vocation, public role, authority, reputation, legacy.
- **themes:** ambition, achievement, status, how the world sees you.

## House 11
- **area:** community, friends, hopes, networks, the collective.
- **themes:** belonging to groups, ideals, shared causes, future vision.

## House 12
- **area:** the unconscious, retreat, transcendence, what is hidden/undone.
- **themes:** solitude, dissolution, spirituality, the unseen and unresolved.
