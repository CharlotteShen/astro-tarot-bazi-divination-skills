# Relationship Frameworks — what to weight by relationship type

Raw material for synastry synthesis, NOT lines to recite. Each type lists which inter-aspects and
house overlays to weight most, and how to frame them. The same contact means different things across
types — read it through the chosen lens.

## Romantic
- **Weight most:** Sun–Moon, Venus–Mars, Moon–Moon, Venus–Venus, Ascendant contacts; Saturn for staying-power; Pluto for intensity.
- **Key overlays:** planets in the 5th (romance/play), 7th (partnership), 8th (intimacy/merging).
- **Frame as:** attraction, emotional safety, desire, friction, and the growth each person sparks in the other.

## Friendship
- **Weight most:** Mercury, Jupiter, Sun–Moon, Moon–Mercury, Uranus for spark.
- **Key overlays:** planets in the 3rd (everyday rapport), 11th (shared vision/community), 5th (fun).
- **Frame as:** rapport, shared interests, freedom, loyalty.

## Family
- **Weight most:** Moon, Saturn, Sun–Moon, IC/Moon contacts, Pluto for inherited patterns.
- **Key overlays:** planets in the 4th (home/roots), 10th (authority), 8th (shared resources/legacy).
- **Frame as:** security, duty, belonging, inherited dynamics met consciously.

## Work
- **Weight most:** Mercury, Saturn, Mars, Sun–Saturn, Mars–Saturn.
- **Key overlays:** planets in the 6th (daily work), 10th (goals/authority), 2nd (resources).
- **Frame as:** productivity, reliability, authority, complementary skills and friction.
