# Synastry Method

Two-chart relationship reading, grounded in `synastry_data`. Obeys all v1 guardrails plus the
synastry guardrails below. Read through the chosen relationship type (`relationship-frameworks.md`).

## Naming & perspective (default)
Refer to the two people by **name**, fixed up front — e.g. "**A = <person_a name>, B = <person_b name>**"
(use "Person A" / "Person B" only if a name is missing). Use the names (A/B) consistently for BOTH sides.
**Do NOT mix "you" with "he/she"** — it's ambiguous about who is who. Switch to **"you-perspective"**
(write from one person's side: "you" = that person, the other by name) ONLY when the user asks; the skill
confirms which chart is the user's first (see `SKILL.md`).

## Stages
1. **Setup.** Confirm both charts are loaded and which relationship type applies (romantic /
   friendship / family / work). If the type wasn't given, ask before reading.
2. **Inter-aspect synthesis.** First, **lead with `double_whammies`** ("this theme is doubly wired —
   it keeps re-asserting itself") and **name the `focal_points`** ("your `<X>` is a hotspot they light
   up several ways"). Then surface the other LOUDEST cross-contacts — tightest orbs, luminaries
   (Sun/Moon), Venus–Mars, Saturn, and Ascendant/MC contacts — weighted by the relationship type.
   Weave ≥2 factors per claim; never a bare list. This is the chemistry and the friction.
3. **House overlays.** What life areas each person activates in the other (both directions:
   `a_in_b` and `b_in_a`), tuned by type. E.g. "their Sun in your 7th — they feel like a partner."
4. **Composite.** Read the relationship as its own entity: composite Sun, Moon, Ascendant and the
   tightest composite aspects = its character and purpose.
5. **Integration.** Name the gifts, the friction, and the growth edges. Two-sided. Both people have
   agency. No verdict, no score.

## Synastry guardrails
- No compatibility score or percentage; no "perfect match," "soulmate," or "doomed."
- Describe a dynamic, not a verdict on a person; don't pathologize either party.
- Hard contacts (e.g. Saturn square, Mars–Pluto) are growth/friction to work with, not fate.
- Both people have free will; describe tendencies, not outcomes.
- Never reference a contact, overlay, or composite factor that is not in `synastry_data`.
- Inherit all v1 guardrails: synthesize ≥2 factors, ~70% plain / 30% technical, empower, no
  medical/legal/financial/death claims.

## Language
Same as the natal skill: default from person A's `birth-data.md` `language` field; switch only on
explicit request. Use `glossary.md` for terms, plus: synastry 合盘 / 比较盘, composite 组合中点盘,
house overlay 宫位叠加, inter-aspect 跨盘相位.
